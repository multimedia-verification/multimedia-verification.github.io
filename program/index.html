Invited Talk (Zoom)

Speaker: Hendrik B. Vold, Associate Director, Norwegian Institute of Journalism.

Vold teaches applied generative AI for journalism, with a strong focus on OSINT and combating disinformation. He has developed workflows for fast verification of media content to provide trusted information for newsrooms, and has run national and global workshops for investigative journalists. He will discuss experiences assessing new LLM workflows for automated verification.
